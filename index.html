HOW XKRO WORKS

SECURE TRANSACTIONS MADE SIMPLE

Xkro offers a secure way to facilitate transactions for physical goods, digital goods, & services.

OPTION 1

Direct Marketplace Purchase

  • 1

    Browse the Marketplace

    Start by browsing the marketplace for a product you're interested in.

  • 2

    Make an Offer

    Once you find a product, make an offer. If the seller agrees, the deal will be initiated.

  • 3

    Product Information

    The seller will list the product with pre-selected details, including whether it's a physical good, its category, and other relevant information.

  • 4

    Transaction Begins

    Once the offer is accepted, the transaction is officially started.

OPTION 2

Using Xkro for Any Transaction

  • 1

    Choose Product Type

    Select the type of transaction — whether it's a physical good, digital good, or a service.

  • 2

    Select Category & Details

    Choose the relevant category for the product or service and provide all required details such as price.

  • 3

    Secure Escrow Payment

    Funds are held securely in Xkro escrow until both parties confirm the deal is complete.

  • 4

    Deal Complete

    Once confirmed, payment is released to the seller. Safe, simple, and transparent.

FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Xkro?

Xkro is a financial arrangement where a third party temporarily holds money or property until a transaction is completed as agreed.

Can I use Xkro for both physical and digital goods?

Yes! Xkro supports transactions for both physical products (e.g., electronics, clothing) and digital goods (e.g., software, online services).

What happens if there is a dispute?

If there is a dispute, our support team will assist both parties in reaching a fair resolution.

How do I know my money is safe?

Your funds are held securely in Xkro until both parties are satisfied with the transaction. Xkro provides a transparent and secure environment.

How long does it take for a transaction to complete?

The transaction time depends on the delivery of goods and buyer's confirmation. Typically, physical product transactions take 7-21 business days, while digital products are instant.
