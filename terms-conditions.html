5. Escrow Services

Xkro acts as an escrow agent for transactions between Buyers and Sellers. Our services include:

  • Holding Escrowed Funds in a non-interest-bearing deposit account
  • Processing payments and disbursements
  • Providing transaction management services
  • Facilitating dispute resolution between parties

5.1 Escrow Transaction Process

  1. Buyer and Seller agree to transaction terms
  2. Buyer deposits funds into escrow
  3. Seller ships Goods or transfers ownership
  4. Buyer inspects and accepts Goods
  5. Xkro releases funds to Seller (less applicable fees)

5.2 Escrowed Funds

Xkro will hold Escrowed Funds in a non-interest-bearing deposit account. Subject to fulfillment of the Escrow Services, We shall be free to invest or utilize the Escrow Funds in any manner we deem fit, without any prior intimation to, or consent of, the Parties.

6. Inspection Period

During the Inspection Period, Buyers and Sellers must inspect the subject property as applicable:

6.1 Buyer's Rights

  • The Buyer shall have 10 days (or such other period as agreed between the Parties) from the date of receipt of the Goods to inspect and verify the same
  • Within the Inspection Period, the Buyer may either accept the Goods or reject them by giving notice of rejection through the App
  • If the Buyer does not respond within the Inspection Period, the Goods shall be deemed to have been accepted

6.2 Rejection Process

  • Upon rejection, the Buyer must provide detailed reasons for rejection
  • The Seller shall have 5 days to respond to the rejection
  • If the parties cannot resolve the dispute, it shall be referred to Xkro for resolution

7. Dispute Resolution

Xkro provides dispute resolution services for transactions conducted through the App. In case of any dispute:

7.1 Dispute Filing

  • A party must file a dispute through the App within the Inspection Period
  • The dispute must include detailed information about the issue
  • Both parties will be notified of the dispute

7.2 Resolution Process

  • Xkro will review all evidence submitted by both parties
  • We may request additional information or documentation
  • Xkro's decision on the dispute shall be final and binding

Note: Xkro reserves the right to hold or refund Escrowed Funds based on our assessment of the dispute. Our decision shall be final and binding on all parties.
